ABOUT US

Read Something About Blood
Donation

We solve the problem of blood emergencies by connecting blood donors directly with people in blood need.

blood donation is a not-for-profit organisation; therefore, ownership of its assets does not vest in its members. In the event that SANBS should stop operating, its assets would have to be transferred to an organisation with similar goals and objectives.

blood donation provides an essential service within South Africa and is rated amongst the best in the world in the provision of blood and blood products, as well as in relation to the research and training provided. Blood donation operates across all of South Africa, with the exclusion of the Western Cape
